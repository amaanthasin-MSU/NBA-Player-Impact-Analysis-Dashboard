# 🏀 NBA Player Impact Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Problem

In basketball, individual player performance is often highlighted, but it is not always clear how much a superstar actually contributes to overall team success. Understanding this relationship is key for evaluating team construction and performance.

---

## 🎯 Objective

Analyze the impact of individual player performance on team success using advanced NBA metrics and visual analytics.

---

## 📊 Dataset

* NBA player and team statistics across multiple seasons
* Metrics include:

  * Player Efficiency Rating (PER)
  * True Shooting Percentage (TS%)
  * Assist Percentage (AST%)
  * Usage Rate (USG%)
  * Win Shares (WS)

---

## ⚙️ Approach

### Data Preparation

* Cleaned and structured player and team performance data
* Selected key metrics to represent player efficiency and contribution

### Analysis

* Compared superstar performance vs supporting cast contributions
* Evaluated trends in team success relative to player efficiency
* Analyzed team rankings alongside individual player metrics

### Visualization

* Bar charts comparing player vs team contributions
* Radar charts for player performance profiles
* Time-series trends of team performance and rankings

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* High individual performance does not always guarantee team success
* Strong supporting casts play a critical role in overall team performance
* Teams with balanced contributions tend to outperform those reliant on a single star
* Metrics like PER and TS% provide valuable insight but must be analyzed in team context

---

This project demonstrates how sports analytics can be used to evaluate player impact beyond traditional statistics, emphasizing the importance of team dynamics alongside individual performance.
